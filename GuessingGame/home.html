Guess The Number

    Rules

    1) You can guess as many times as you'd like until you guess the correct number.


    2) Your guess must be a 4 digit number.


    3) Partial means that you are partially correct, but one or more of the numbers is not in the correct spot.


    4) Exact means that one or more of the numbers is correct.


    5) You can exit out of a game anytime by clicking the "Back To Main" button.
